02 · Purchase Psychology

What makes her buy. What makes her scroll.

Buy Triggers

  • The kid's reaction. "My son is thrilled when it came in the mail." She is buying the moment, and the glow reveal is a moment she can film.
  • Back to school deadline. The Crocs restock video that converted at the highest rate opened with a sellout warning tied to back to school. Urgency works in this category.
  • Practical cover. Padded laptop sleeve, front organizer, 13.5" x 17". She needs a reason this is a school bag and not merch. The product gives her three.
  • Sits under the Crocs price. She has seen the $97.66 bag all over her feed. $49.99 reads like the smart version of the same purchase.

Objections

  • The $39.99 bundles. Fimibuke sells backpack, lunch box, and pencil case together and moved about $431K across four SKUs in 30 days. Content has to beat the bundle math with the glow and the brand.
  • One colorway. Black only. Crocs sells personalization as the headline. Position black as the daytime disguise the glow hides inside.
  • Does the glow actually work? The effect needs a light charge. Show the charge and the payoff on camera or the comments will ask.
  • Durability. 100% polyester and a school year of abuse. Reviews already answer this. Use them.
