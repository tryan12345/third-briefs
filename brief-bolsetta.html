01 · The Winning Formula

Non-negotiables

Six rules from the converting record across both Bolsetta handles. Break these and the video gets views instead of orders. The proof: the best converting video did $25.00 per 1,000 views on 491K reach. The biggest video did 1.1M views and converted at $5.64 per 1,000. Reach is not the assignment. 01

Snap it on in the first 3 seconds

Waistband, one motion, on camera. The snap is the mechanic no fanny pack or MagSafe wallet can copy. A video without it is a generic pouch ad.

02

Load it on camera

Phone, cards, keys, cash, zip. "What fits" is the buyer's first question and the workhorse shot of every Bolsetta video that sold.

03

Move in it

Walk, bend, reach. The airport review sold it in one line: "not one time did it move." Prove that sentence with footage.

04

Zip a big phone inside

The #1 return reason on the TikTok listing is "my phone didn't fit," including an iPhone 15 Pro Max. Put a Pro Max class phone in, zip it shut, on camera, and name the model out loud. One shot kills the return.

05

Say the price

$37.62 on TikTok Shop, said out loud. Under $40 is the impulse line for this buyer, and free shipping closes the math. The price is an argument. Make it.

06

Film it ad-clean

Every Bolsetta video that made real money has ad spend on it. Original voice, minimal text overlay, no borrowed audio. Your video has to survive as an ad, because that is the plan for it.

Do

  • Show it on snug leggings. A reviewer confirms it "works super well with leggings" and another warns loose waistbands sag under the weight. Show the right way and say why.
  • Use the buyer's words. "Very strong magnetic hold so it doesn't move." "It fits her giant phone." "I can even sit while wearing it." The reviews wrote the script.
  • Show two colorways. Black plus one of Sandstone, Seafoam Teal, Lavender, or Navy. The quilt look is the reason this SKU exists.
  • Keep it 20 to 40 seconds. One product, one point, out. The winners are tight demos, not vlogs.

Don't

  • Don't call it a fanny pack. There is no belt. That is the entire point. Wrong word, wrong mental picture, lost sale.
  • Don't open with a greeting. Every top converter opens on the product in motion, not a face saying hi.
  • Don't chase views without selling. The 1.1M-view video converted at a fifth of the best rate. Demo first, entertainment second.
  • Don't skip the tag or the link. Untagged videos sell zero units and cannot be boosted. No exceptions.
