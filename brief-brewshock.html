#1 thing buyers ask · 04 / 06

Product cheat sheet

Buyers ask two things first: will it make me jittery, and is it actually strong enough. Answer both in plain language. Here’s the translation table.

Caffeine + L-theanine
Say: “Smooth focus, no jitters.” This pairing is the whole pitch.
Coffee fruit extract + theobromine
Say: “It doesn’t drop you at 2pm.” The slow-release answer to the strength doubt.
Zero sugar, MCT + collagen
Say: “No sugar, and it’s actually got something in it.” Bonus line, not the lead.

Confirm claim language with the brand before filming. Describe the feel, never promise a medical outcome.
